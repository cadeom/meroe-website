What changes with cadeom

Software that puts the review first.

01

Cycle Time

Every stage of a review, tracked in real time.

Know the status of any review, who or what is blocking it, and how cycle times trend across your teams and project types. Insights available from every stage.

"Which reviewers consistently catch critical issues? Which document types generate the most comments?"

02

Resolution Rate

Structured comments that carry forward across revisions.

Comments are categorised and templated the moment they are raised, and traceable from creation through response to closeout. Document originators respond in context, and unresolved comments transfer to the next revision automatically, so that resolution happens inside the process.

"Traceable from creation through response through closeout."

03

Institutional Knowledge

Review results captured as data, not PDFs in email.

Every closed review feeds the next. Over time cadeom builds a picture of your review quality, where issues cluster and where reviewers excel, and AI surfaces relevant patterns and flags likely issues, to make professional judgement sharper.

"That knowledge exists, it just never gets captured in a form anyone can act on."
